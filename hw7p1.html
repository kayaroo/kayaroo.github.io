This is the first paragraph. The first paragraph explains how the customization of the background color and highlighting of the text works. The first of the options to customize the text will give you the ability to highlight the paragraphs into whatever color you want. There are five colors to choose from. Pink, blue, green, yellow, and purple. Whatever color you select from these buttons will highlight the text in that color.

This is the second paragraph. The second paragraph explains how customizing the text style of the paragraphs works. This option of customization allows the user to change how they want the text to be presented. This gives a total of three choices to choose from. Italic, Underline, and Bold. You are allowed to choose multiple of the checkboxes at the same time. You can have one checkbox or two, or all of them be selected at the same time. This can be undone by unchecking the box. Pressing the checkbox next to the bold option makes the text of the paragraphs bold. Pressing the checkbox next to the italic option makes the text change into having italic text. Pressing the checkmark next to underline option causes the text to be underlined.

This is the third paragraph. The third paragraph explains how to change the size of the font of the paragraph text and how they work. There are three options to choose from in the drop box. There's small, medium and large.
